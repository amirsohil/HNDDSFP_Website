Background

This project was undertaken to fulfil partial requirements of the Higher National Diploma in Data Science programme offered by the National Institute of Business Management, Sri Lanka in collaboration with Coventry University, UK.

An overview of the major topics and findings in relation to the project would be discussed in this website.
